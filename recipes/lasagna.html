Lasagna

This is a lasagna recipe

My mother used to make me this lasagna, it is so yuummmyy

This lasagna should take you around 30 minutes to do, it's an easy recipe to follow. You will not be disapointed!!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Assemble
  4. Cook in the oven at 300 celsius for 50 minutes
