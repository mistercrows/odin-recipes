Greek Salad

This is a Greek Salad recipe

My mother used to make me this Greek Salad, it is so yuummmyy

This greek salad should take you around 30 minutes to do, it's an easy recipe to follow. You will not be disapointed!!

Ingredients

  • cucumbers
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Kalamata olives
  • 3 tbs dressing

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients
  2. Add the dressing
